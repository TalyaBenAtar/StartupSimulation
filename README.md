# 🚀 Startup Simulation

> **Can you build the next billion-dollar startup?**

Startup Simulation is a business management simulation game developed entirely in **SwiftUI** for iOS. The game places you in the role of a startup founder, challenging you to build a successful company from the ground up by making strategic business decisions, managing employees, investing in growth, and surviving the unpredictable nature of the startup world.

Every decision has consequences. Spend too aggressively and you'll run out of money. Play too safely and competitors will leave you behind. Balance growth with sustainability and maybe—just maybe—you'll build the next Unicorn.

# 🌍 About the Game

Every great company begins with a single founder willing to take a risk.

In Startup Simulation, you start with your own personal wealth and invest part of it into creating a brand-new startup. From that moment on, every month becomes a series of strategic decisions that determine whether your company thrives or collapses.

Throughout the game you will:

- Build and grow your company
- Invest in product development
- Market your business
- Hire and fire employees
- Manage recurring expenses
- Improve your company's valuation
- Respond to unexpected business events
- Decide whether to keep growing or sell your company

There is **no single winning strategy**. Different investment styles can all lead to success—or failure.

---

# 🎯 Goal

Your objective is to build the most successful startup possible.

To achieve that, you'll need to carefully balance several aspects of your business:

- 💰 Cash
- 📈 Market Value
- 🛠 Product Quality
- 😊 Employee Morale
- 📢 Brand Awareness
- 👥 Team Size
- 📊 Monthly Revenue

Growing one area while neglecting another can quickly lead to financial problems.

---

# 🎮 How to Play

Every month follows the same gameplay loop.

---

## 📊 Step 1 — Review Your Company

At the beginning of every month you'll receive a complete overview of your startup.

Monitor your:

- Current cash
- Monthly revenue
- Company valuation
- Product quality
- Employee morale
- Brand awareness
- Number of employees
- Monthly expenses

Understanding your company's current condition is essential before making any investments.

---

## 🛠 Step 2 — Improve Your Product

A better product creates a stronger company.

Investing in Product Development permanently increases your product quality, making your startup more valuable over time and improving future company growth.

The challenge is deciding **how much** to invest while still leaving enough cash to survive.

---

## 📢 Step 3 — Market Your Startup

A great product is useless if nobody knows it exists.

Marketing increases your company's visibility and future revenue.

Choose between:

- One-time marketing campaigns
- Recurring monthly marketing subscriptions

Aggressive marketing can accelerate growth—but also dramatically increase monthly expenses.

---

## 👥 Step 4 — Build Your Team

No startup succeeds alone.

Hire employees to strengthen your company.

Each employee contributes to company growth while increasing salary expenses.

You can also fire employees if reducing expenses becomes necessary.

Managing the balance between productivity and payroll is one of the game's biggest challenges.

---

## 💰 Step 5 — Manage Your Finances

Every month your startup earns revenue—but it also spends money.

Recurring expenses include:

- Employee salaries
- Operating expenses
- Marketing subscriptions

One-time expenses include:

- Product investments
- Marketing campaigns
- Hiring costs
- Severance payments

Your cash balance determines whether your startup can continue operating.

---

## 🎲 Step 6 — Face Random Business Events

The startup world is unpredictable.

At the beggining of each month your company may encounter unexpected situations such as:

- 🚀 Viral publicity
- 💼 Investor interest
- 💰 Government grants
- 🔒 Security vulnerabilities
- 😓 Employee burnout
- 📉 Economic recession
- 🤖 AI productivity breakthroughs
- 🏢 Enterprise opportunities
- 🌊 Office disasters
- ⚔ Competition

Every event presents multiple choices.

Each decision comes with different risks and rewards, making every playthrough unique.

---

## 📅 Step 7 — Advance to the Next Month

Once your decisions are complete, the monthly simulation begins.

The game automatically recalculates:

- Cash flow
- Revenue
- Market value
- Product quality
- Employee morale
- Brand awareness

Your company then moves into the following month, where the cycle begins again.

---

# 🏆 Winning & Losing

Your startup can end in one of three different ways.

---

## 🦄 Unicorn

Reach a market valuation of **$1,000,000,000** and officially become a Unicorn startup.

Congratulations—you've built one of the world's most valuable companies.

---

## 💰 Acquisition

Receive and accept an acquisition offer.

Selling your startup adds the acquisition value to your founder wealth, allowing larger investments into future startups.

---

## 💀 Bankruptcy

If your company exceeds the maximum debt limit, your startup goes bankrupt.

Growing too quickly without enough financial stability can be just as dangerous as not growing at all.
